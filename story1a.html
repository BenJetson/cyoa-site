You are in the Calvert club. You see Giuseppe Zangara setting there sipping on his wine. You sit there mulling over whether or not to confront him. “What if it rips apart the fabric of time” you ponder aloud. You decided to sit down and have a drink to calm your nerves. You can see yourself shaking. Giuseppe looks like he’s about ready to leave so what do you do? Do you go talk to him or stay a while and order food?
