When you read the letter you decide not to go to the warehouse because you think something bad might happen. You are confused on how the letter got in the car and do not really trust the whole situation. So instead you decide to take what you think is the safe option and disregard the letter and drive home.

It is an extremely rainy night, rain was coming down so hard that you can barely see the road ahead of you. Also, you are out later than usual because you stayed at work later than usual to get some work done. You have almost lost control a couple of times. Then you go around a turn too fast and lose total control of the car. You wreck into a ditch on the side of the road and die.
