You do what the note says and go to the warehouse. It is dark and dreary when you arrive at the warehouse. You walk into the warehouse soaked because of the pouring rain. A man is sitting by what looks to be a time machine, eating a sandwich. He greets you like y’all have been friends forever but you don’t recognize him. He introduces himself as, Dr. Epings. He invites you to sit with him and then goes on to explain what the time machine is and how he created such a machine. You ask him why exactly he brought you here. Dr. Epings tells you that he wants you to go on a mission to save your great grandfather, Theodore Roosevelt, from Giuseppe. At first you are hesitant to accept the offer, then Dr. Epings is able to persuade you to enter the time machine and be a hero.
